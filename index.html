🌱 Our Story

Born in Nanded,
Made for Everyone

Zorko Nanded is more than a fast-food outlet — it's a community destination. Nestled near the heart of Nanded at Near Bhagya Nagar, we bring the bold flavours and exciting energy of the Zorko brand directly to Maharashtra's streets.

We believe that incredible food shouldn't break the bank. Every item on our menu is crafted daily with fresh ingredients, bold sauces, and genuine care — from our iconic Mexican King Burger to our showstopping Sizzling Brownie.

Good food brings people together. We're here to make every bite worth the trip to Zorko Nanded.
